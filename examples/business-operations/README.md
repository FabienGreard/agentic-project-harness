# Business Operations preset

Management acts as **Program Director**: owns program outcomes, priorities, scope, stakeholder decisions, and consequential approvals.

Operations acts as **Operations Manager**: turns direction into an executable operating plan and owns coordination, implementation, controls, and evidence.

The recommended starting Consultant is **Change Manager**, responsible for defining and accepting adoption, communication, training, readiness, and transition evidence. The installer also offers Compliance Officer, Financial Controller, Legal Counsel, Risk Manager, People Operations Lead, Data Governance Lead, and Quality Manager. Select any number or none.

Operations may dispatch disposable Contractors for process and policy, operations implementation, or assurance and analysis. Those capability labels guide routing; they are not permanent user-managed roles.

Users still speak to Management, Operations, Consultants, and Contractors. The professional titles supply domain context without changing authority.
