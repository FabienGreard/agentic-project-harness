# Business-operations adaptation

Use this overlay for internal operations, service delivery, policy, finance, compliance, research, or customer-facing business programs.

## Suggested role names

- Project Director → Program Director, Product Owner, or Executive Sponsor
- Delivery Lead → Operations Lead, Program Manager, or Implementation Lead
- Specialist Lead → Finance, Legal, Compliance, Security, Data, HR, Clinical, Tax, or another recurring expert authority
- Execution workers → analysts, researchers, process designers, data workers, document workers, or system implementers

## Domain readiness

Define affected stakeholders, current process, target outcome, measurable service levels, data sources, controls, legal/privacy basis, operational owner, rollback/recovery, training, and monitoring proportional to risk.

Specialist review does not replace operational verification. A legally acceptable policy may still be unusable; an efficient process may still be noncompliant. Require both where applicable.

## Human gates

Explicit approval may be required before financial commitments, customer communication, policy publication, employment action, regulated submission, access-control change, data migration, or irreversible operational rollout.

## Useful workers

Parallelize independent research streams, data validation, documentation, training material, and non-overlapping process components after contracts stabilize. Serialize decisions that change shared policy, source-of-truth data, financial records, or external commitments.
