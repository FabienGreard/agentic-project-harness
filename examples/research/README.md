# Research preset

Management acts as **Principal Investigator**: owns the research question, scientific direction, priorities, scope, integrity, and consequential approvals.

Operations acts as **Research Program Manager**: turns research direction into an executable program and owns coordination, resources, dependencies, evidence, and delivery.

The recommended starting Consultant is **Research Methodologist**, responsible for defining and accepting methodological fit, validity, study design, and evidential limits. The installer also offers Statistician, Research Ethics Lead, Data Steward, Reproducibility Lead, Subject-Matter Expert, and Computational Research Lead. Select any number or none.

Operations may dispatch disposable Contractors for evidence and literature, data and methods, or reproduction and reporting. Those capability labels guide routing; they are not permanent user-managed roles.

Users still speak to Management, Operations, Consultants, and Contractors. The professional titles supply domain context without changing authority.
