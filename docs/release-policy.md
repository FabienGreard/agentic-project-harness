# Publication and release policy

Customize this policy to the project's domain. “Release” may mean software deployment, public document publication, customer communication, financial submission, campaign launch, or another externally consequential action.

- Management is the default publication owner unless direction explicitly assigns another human-controlled role.
- Operations returns verified integrated state but does not publish merely because work is complete.
- Human approval is required at every boundary declared in `docs/direction.md` or the controlling ticket.
- Preserve coherent internal checkpoints without presenting them as external approval or release.
- Record version/change history where the domain benefits from it.
- Never publish secrets, private task identifiers, unapproved personal data, or unrelated worktree changes.
